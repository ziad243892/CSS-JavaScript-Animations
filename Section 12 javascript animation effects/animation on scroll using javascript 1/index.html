Meet Oreo 🐾

Oreo is not your average cat. With his warm, hazelnut-brown fur and wise, soulful eyes

Oreo has been a beloved companion

and quiet observer of life’s little moments for many years

Medium in size but large in personality

he brings a calm, comforting presence wherever he goes

Whether he’s lounging in his favorite sunspot

supervising creative projects, or sneaking a curious glance at the camera
